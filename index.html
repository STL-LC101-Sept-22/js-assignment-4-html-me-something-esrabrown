MY SOLO TRIP TO JAMAICA

In 2019, I decided to take a solo trip to Jamaica. I wanted to use Couchsurfing for a week. I was lucky that I found an amazing host who helped me a lot in Kingston. Then, I took a bus to visit the beautiful city Ocho Rios which is 2 hours from Kingston.

Jamaica is the land of wood and water, waterfalls, rivers, mountains, and more. I explored all the ares that the Ocho Rios region had to offer. Each time I became more and more spiritually connected to a place I had never visited before. Ocho Rios is Spanish name, which translates to “Eight Rivers.”

The local people I met through CouchSurfing helped me a lot during my trip. They took care of my belongings when I visited Ocho Rios for few days. They showed me places to exchange my money, where to eat the best ice-cream in Jamaica, and how to take a bus to visit Bob Marley's museum. I did not have internet during my trip, so having someone I could trust and rely on for information and direction helped make my trip safe and enjoyable.

Jamaican locals are a very friendly and proud of their culture. They are always ready to help to make your vacation unforgettable. I made some friends from there and we still talk today. I will never forget my Jamaica trip and I am looking forward to visting my friends over there again someday.
