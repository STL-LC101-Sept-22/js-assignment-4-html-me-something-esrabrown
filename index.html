MY SOLO TRIP TO JAMAICA

In 2019, I decided to take a solo trip to Jamaica. I used Couchsurfing when I was traveling in Spain and I wanted to use it in Jamaica for a week. I was lucky that I found an amazing host who helped me a lot. They took care of my documents such of my passport and some money, they gave me their backpack to take a bus to visit another city for few days because it is not safe to carry important stuff. When I was outside walking, bunch of people was walking with me, asking me questions and staring at me. To be honest I was a bit scared. But I can clearly say that Jamaican people love helping. I made it safe and enjoyed a lot, made some friends that I still talk. I never forget my Jamaica trip and looking forward to visit my friends over there.
